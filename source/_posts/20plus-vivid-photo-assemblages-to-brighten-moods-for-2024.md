#### Themes

It is always advisable to create a photo collage based on a theme. Choose photos that best describe your chosen image. For instance; if you are creating a collage for your brotherâ€™s birthday, you can choose snapshots highlighting the special events in his life.

#### Layout

#### Cohesion

Ultimately, the major difference between an artful collage and a collection of photos is visual cohesion. Although this might sound like a lofty artistic undertaking, itâ€™s quite simple. Select photos with similar color schemes for creating a collage with a unified feel.
